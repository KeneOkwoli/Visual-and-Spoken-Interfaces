Speech Recognition Commands:

"Go Forward" - Moves Forward
"Retreat" - Moves Backwards
"Stop" - Stops the robot
"Object Recognition" - Prints the distance of an object in front of the car (needs to be connected to the PC)
"Obstacle" - Checks if an object is too close in front.(YES --> Head Nodding, NO --> Head Shaking)
"Wander Around" - Moves freely and uses the three sensors to choose which direction it should go in.
